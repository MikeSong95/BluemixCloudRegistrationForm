Registration


To qualify for funding please complete the following online form.

We will be asking for proof of income to make sure you qualify for our program. The qualifying number is a total household income of $45,000 and less. Documentation will be required. Your 2014 Notice of Assessment is required. When prompted in the application forms please attach this document.

Please Note:

All scholarships are based on available funds. Please ensure you apply at least one moth prior to your sport/ recreation activity start date. You must be registered with the sport or recreation activity organization you have chosen to be eligible for funding. You must select a location to complete your Give Back in order to qualify for funding.

To begin please select your area or location you would like funding for.
